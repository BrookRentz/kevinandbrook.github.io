Labor Day in the Mountains

September 2018

Kevin’s September business trip to the Denver office pulled double duty. We spent Labor Day weekend relaxing at Boulder Brook on Fall River in Estes Park, CO.

And by “relaxing” I, of course, mean hiking in Rocky Mountain National Park!

We drove the old Fall River Road to Trail Ridge Road and hiked on both sides of the park. Our favorite hike in the park is still the Bear Lake, Nymph Lake, Dream Lake, Emerald Lake hike.

We ended with a day in Denver. Kevin had to work, so I spent the day at the Denver Botanical Garden.
